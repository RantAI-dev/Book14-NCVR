<p style="text-align: justify;">
<em>Part II of "NCVR - Numerical Computing via Rust" develops the core machinery for solving linear systems and approximating functions. It covers direct and iterative approaches to linear algebra, techniques for interpolation and numerical approximation, methods for numerical integration and quadrature, and strategies for evaluating and approximating both general and special functions. These topics form a shared toolkit that recurs throughout numerical computing, and the emphasis here is on understanding the underlying principles well enough to reason about accuracy, stability, and efficiency when implementing them in Rust.</em>
