The book is organized into eight parts, each delving into theoretical foundations, algorithmic implementations, and real-world applications across various domains of numerical computation. Whether you are a student, researcher, or professional, NCVR provides a comprehensive roadmap to harnessing Rust for precise, scalable, and trustworthy numerical software.

### **Guidance for Students and Lecturers**

<p style="text-align: justify;">
<strong>For Students:</strong> Use this book as a comprehensive resource for mastering numerical computing and its practical implementation in Rust. Begin with the foundational chapter in Part I and progressively build your skills through the advanced topics in subsequent parts. Engage actively with the hands-on exercises and implementations provided to reinforce your learning.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
<strong>For Lecturers:</strong> "NCVR - Numerical Computing via Rust" serves as an excellent primary or supplementary textbook for courses in numerical methods and scientific computing. Its well-structured progression from theory to practice enables you to create engaging assignments, laboratory sessions, and discussion topics that illuminate both the mathematical and programming aspects of the field.
</p>
