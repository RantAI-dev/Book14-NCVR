<p style="text-align: justify;">
<em>Part VI of "NCVR - Numerical Computing via Rust" concerns the modeling of data and the drawing of inferences from it. It moves from the statistical description of data, through the fitting of models to observations, to the tasks of classification and inference. These chapters connect numerical computation with statistics and data analysis, providing the tools to summarize measurements, estimate parameters, and reason about uncertainty. The emphasis is on understanding the assumptions behind common methods and implementing them soundly in Rust so that conclusions drawn from data are well founded.</em>
